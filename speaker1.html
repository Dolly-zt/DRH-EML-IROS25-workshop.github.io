Qi Ye

Researcher under the 'Hundred Talents Program' and a Doctoral Supervisor-Zhejiang University.

Keynote Title

Presentation Topic 1

Bio

At the end of October 2020, she joined the "Network Sensing and Control" research group led by Academician Sun Youxian and Professor Chen Jiming at the Institute of Industrial Control, College of Control Science and Engineering, Zhejiang University. She was appointed as a researcher under the "Hundred Talents Program" at Zhejiang University and a doctoral supervisor. Before joining Zhejiang University, she obtained her undergraduate degree from Beijing Normal University in 2011, her master's degree from Tsinghua University in 2014, and her Ph.D. degree from Imperial College London, UK in 2019 (supervised by Tae - kyun Kim). In January 2019, she joined the Mixed Reality & AI Lab of Microsoft in Cambridge, UK, engaging in research and development related to 3D human body reconstruction and tracking, as well as VR/AR. She participated in the development of the gesture - tracking algorithm for Microsoft's new - generation augmented reality glasses HoloLens 2. Her main collaborators included Andrew Fitzgibbon, Jamie Shotton, Toby Sharp, Tom Cashman, etc.
